Partnership Program

About Partnership Program

With zero investment and unlimited earning potential, join the SubmitZ Partner Program and unlock new business opportunities while earning attractive remuneration for every successful referral, lead, or customer acquisition. SubmitZ is a modern travel and expense management software that helps organizations automate expense tracking, approvals, reimbursements, and reporting.

We invite consultants, software companies, resellers, accounting firms, HR agencies, business advisors, system integrators, technology partners, and independent professionals to partner with us and grow together.

As a SubmitZ Partner, you can introduce businesses to a powerful expense management solution while generating a recurring revenue stream and strengthening your client relationships with value-added services.

Step
