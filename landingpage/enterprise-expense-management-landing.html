The Problem At Scale

What basic expense tools quietly cost a growing enterprise

None of these problems are visible in a 50-person pilot. All of them become very visible at 500.

Fragmentation

Five tools, one process

Travel booked in one app, claims filed in another, approvals over email, reconciliation in Excel — nothing talks to anything else.

Inconsistency

Policy enforced by memory

Without a single rules engine, the same claim gets approved in one branch and rejected in another — based on who happens to review it.

Blind spots

Reporting after the fact

Spend visibility arrives in a month-end spreadsheet, long after the budget has already been exceeded.
