Built For Every Employee

What makes employee expense management actually work

It's not enough to digitize a form. SubmitZ removes friction at every step so employees actually use it, and finance can trust it.

Mobile-First Submission

Employees submit claims, attach receipts and check status from the SubmitZ app on Android or iOS — no laptop required.

Grade-Wise Policies

Set spend limits and eligible categories by designation, grade or location so policy is fair and automatic, not manual judgment.

Multi-Level Approvals

Workflows follow your real reporting hierarchy, with partial approvals, comments and resubmission built in.

Advances That Auto-Adjust

Issue a cash or bank advance before a trip, and SubmitZ automatically reconciles it against the employee's actual claims.

Multi-Lingual Access

Every employee can use SubmitZ in their preferred language — helpful for field staff and non-English-first teams.

Self-Service Status Tracking

Employees see exactly where each claim stands — submitted, approved, processing or reimbursed — without asking anyone.
